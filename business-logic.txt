🏢 Core Modules & Business Logic
1. Property Management
Property Portfolio Overview

List all properties with key metrics: occupancy rate, monthly revenue, pending maintenance.

Map view for geographic distribution.

Detailed Property Record

Address, type (apartment, house, commercial), units, amenities, images/videos.

Documents: deeds, insurance, inspection reports (upload & expiry alerts).

Custom fields for agent companies (e.g., owner contact, commission structure).

Unit Management

For multi‑unit buildings: track each unit separately (size, rent, deposit, status).

Unit history: past tenants, rent changes, maintenance logs.

Availability Calendar

Visual calendar showing vacant/occupied units with future bookings (for short‑term rentals).

2. Tenant Management
Tenant Screening & Onboarding

Online rental applications with customisable forms.

Integrated background/credit check (via third‑party APIs).

Digital lease signing (e‑signature integration).

Tenant Profiles

Contact details, emergency contacts, ID documents, lease history.

Linked leases, payment history, communication log.

Tenant Portals

Self‑service: view lease, pay rent, submit maintenance requests, update contact info.

Real‑time notifications (rent due, maintenance updates).

3. Lease & Contract Management
Lease Templates

Customisable clauses per jurisdiction (supports multiple languages).

Automated lease generation based on property/tenant data.

Lease Lifecycle

Start/end dates, renewal reminders, move‑out checklists.

Security deposit tracking: collection, deductions, return.

Document Storage

Central repository for all contracts, addendums, and correspondence.

4. Rent Collection & Payments
Flexible Payment Methods

Online payments (credit card, ACH/bank transfer) – integration with Stripe, PayPal, etc.

Cash/check recording with receipt generation.

Automated Rent Invoicing

Recurring invoices sent automatically before due date.

Prorated rent for mid‑month move‑ins/outs.

Arrears Management

Automatic late fee calculation (configurable rules).

Overdue reminders (SMS/email) with escalation workflows.

Payment plans for tenants in arrears.

Rent Adjustments

Annual rent increase suggestions based on market data (AI‑powered).

Bulk rent updates for multiple units.

5. Expense Tracking
Expense Categories

Maintenance, repairs, utilities, insurance, property taxes, marketing.

Receipt upload and OCR data extraction.

Recurring Expenses

Set up monthly bills (e.g., cleaning, landscaping) with auto‑categorisation.

Vendor Management

Store vendor details, track payments to contractors.

Profit & Loss per Property

Real‑time calculation of net operating income (NOI), cash flow.

6. Maintenance & Caretakers
Maintenance Requests

Tenant‑submitted requests with photos/urgency levels.

Automatic assignment to caretakers/maintenance staff based on skills/availability.

Caretaker Management

Profiles, schedules, task lists, and completion tracking.

Mobile app for caretakers to receive tasks, update status, upload completion photos.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections (HVAC, smoke detectors, etc.) with reminders.

Inventory Management

Track supplies (paint, bulbs) and reorder alerts.

7. Communication & Notifications
Multi‑channel Communication

In‑app messaging, email, SMS (with templates).

Two‑way communication with tenants and vendors.

Automated Alerts

Rent due reminders, lease expiry, maintenance overdue, document expiry.

Broadcast Messages

Send announcements to all tenants (e.g., water shutdown).

8. Reporting & Analytics
Standard Reports

Rent roll, aged receivables, vacancy report, expense summary.

Tax reports (1099 for contractors, Schedule E for landlords).

Dashboards

Executive dashboard: portfolio health, cash flow, occupancy trends.

Property‑level dashboards with charts.

Custom Reports

Drag‑and‑drop report builder with export (PDF, Excel).

Predictive Analytics

Forecast vacancies, rent collections, and maintenance costs using ML.

9. Compliance & Document Management
Regulatory Compliance

Rent control rules (automatic cap enforcement where applicable).

Habitability standards checklists.

Data retention policies (GDPR/CCPA compliance).

Audit Trail

Log every action (who, what, when) for security and legal purposes.

Digital Signatures

Legally binding e‑signatures for leases, addendums, and consents.

10. Multi‑User Roles & Permissions (for Agencies)
Role‑Based Access Control

Super Admin (SaaS owner), Agency Admin, Property Manager, Accountant, Caretaker, Tenant.

Granular permissions: view only, edit, delete per module.

Team Collaboration

Assign properties to specific team members.

Internal notes and task assignments.

Commission Tracking

For agencies: track commissions per property/owner.

Split payments between owners and agency.
